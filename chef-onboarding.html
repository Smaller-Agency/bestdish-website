Resident shelf price sits in the $20–35 band. We propose the exact price with you from the agreed unit economics — you don't need to set it here.
04Ingredients & allergens

Required for your CFIA-compliant label. List every ingredient in descending order by weight, including sub-ingredients of any prepared component (e.g. sauce, stock).

05Nutrition facts (per serving)

Per the serving size you produce. If you have a lab analysis, attach it in section 8 and you can leave individual fields blank — we'll transcribe from the report.

06Sourcing & farms

We attribute farms and suppliers on the package QR — from farm, to chef, to you. Tell us who grows or makes your key ingredients.

07Copy for your landing page

We write in the BestDish voice, but it starts with your words. Plain, specific, in your voice — we'll shape the rest.

08Photography & logos

Add your dish photo, logo, chef headshot, and lab / nutrition document — these upload with your submission. Large photos are resized automatically; please use a high-resolution original for the logo.

09Finishing & reheat instructions

Residents finish your dish in their own oven. Give us the exact steps so it tastes the way it does in your dining room.
