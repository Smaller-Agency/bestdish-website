How it works

Four steps. From the pass to your plate.

  1. 1

    Made by chefs

    Cooked by the chefs who made them famous — same techniques, same ingredients, same standards as service.

  2. 2

    Flash frozen

    Locked in at peak the moment they leave the kitchen. Flavour, texture, and nutrients stay intact.

  3. 3

    In your building

    A curated freezer of chef meals in your lobby. Tap your card, pick your dish, done.

  4. 4

    Finished by you

    Cooked fresh in your own oven in minutes — exactly the way the chef designed it.
