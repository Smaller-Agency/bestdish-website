How it works

From the kitchen
to your kitchen.

Restaurant meals only work in your building if every step is right. Here's every step.

01 · In the restaurant

Cooked by the chefs
who made it famous.

Every dish is made in the originating restaurant kitchen. Same techniques. Same ingredients. Same standards as service.

02 · At peak

Flash frozen,
minutes from finish.

Each meal is portioned and frozen at the moment it leaves the kitchen. The cold-chain begins at the pass.

03 · Through the cold chain

Tracked, batched,
labelled.

HACCP-compliant cold-chain. CFIA-labelled. Temperature logged from kitchen to freezer.

04 · In your building

A curated freezer
on your floor.

Smart freezer, premium installation. Tap your card. Pick your dish. Telemetry keeps inventory restocked within 48 hours.

05 · In your hand

Take it upstairs.

No delivery driver. No waiting. No tip. No tax. From the freezer to your kitchen in the time it takes to ride the elevator.

06 · In your oven

Cooked fresh,
the way it was designed.

Heating instructions on every package. Per dish. Per restaurant. The chef tells you how to finish their food.
