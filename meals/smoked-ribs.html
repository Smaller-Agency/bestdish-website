Smoked Pork Ribs

St. Louis cut. Slow-smoked over Ontario hardwood. Carbon Bar's house BBQ sauce.

Chef

Adam Hynam-Smith

Serving

1 rack half (400 g)

Net weight

520 g

Calories

740

"Low and slow. That's the whole secret. We finish them with the same sauce we use at the pass." — Adam Hynam-Smith, The Carbon Bar

Heating instructions

Oven

Preheat to 350°F. Remove lid. Heat for 25 minutes.

Microwave

Remove lid. Heat on high for 6–7 minutes.

Keep frozen until use. Do not refreeze after thawing.

Ingredients

St. Louis pork ribs, Dry rub (paprika, brown sugar, salt, garlic, black pepper, mustard, chili), BBQ sauce (tomato, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, molasses, Worcestershire, spices).

Contains: Mustard.

May contain: Wheat, Soy.

Made in Canada with domestic and imported ingredients.
