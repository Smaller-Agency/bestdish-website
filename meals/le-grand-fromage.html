Slowhand

Le Grand Fromage Pizza

A Detroit-style pizza with a thick base, crisp edges, and a soft interior, designed to maintain structure and texture after reheating.

Chef

Brett Feeley & Dan Ewing

Serving

2 cups (300 g)

Calories

800

Category

Savoury

The chef

"Slowhand Pizza was born from an obsession with Detroit-style pizza, sourdough fermentation, and the pursuit of the perfect crispy edge."— Brett Feeley & Dan Ewing, Slowhand

Heating instructions

Oven

Preheat to 425°F. Place directly on the middle rack. Bake 10–12 minutes until cheese is bubbly. Let rest 2 minutes before serving.

Microwave

Not recommended.

Keep frozen until use. Do not refreeze after thawing.

Ingredients

Pizza dough (flour, water), Mozzarella cheese, Pizza sauce (tomatoes, salt, citric acid), Pecorino cheese (sheep milk, salt, lamb rennet, cheese cultures).

Contains: Wheat, Milk.

May contain: Egg, Anchovies, Mustard, Sesame, Soybean, Hazelnut and Pecan.

Made in Canada with domestic and imported ingredients.
