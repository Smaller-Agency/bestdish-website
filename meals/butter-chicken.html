Pukka

Butter Chicken Curry

A classic North Indian dish built on a slow-cooked tomato base, finished with cream and a balanced blend of spices. Designed to hold depth and structure through reheating.

Chef

Dinesh Butola

Serving

2 cups (300 g)

Calories

410

Category

Savoury

The chef

"At Pukka, we believe in real food — thoughtfully prepared, properly cooked, and rooted in tradition. This dish reflects that approach."— Dinesh Butola, Pukka

Heating instructions

Oven

Preheat to 375°F. Remove lid and cover with foil. Bake 20–25 minutes. Remove foil. Let rest 2 minutes before serving.

Microwave

Remove lid. Heat on high 4–6 minutes, stirring halfway. Let rest 2 minutes before serving.

Keep frozen until use. Do not refreeze after thawing.

Ingredients

Basmati rice, Chicken breast, Canned tomato purée, Diced onions, Cream, Canned tomato paste, Plain yogurt (milk, bacteria culture), Butter, Vegetable oil, Garlic, Salt, Lemon juice, Fresh ginger, Sugar, Garam masala, Spices.

Contains: Milk.

May contain: Wheat, Egg, Anchovies, Mustard, Sesame, Soybean, Hazelnut and Pecan.

Made in Canada with domestic and imported ingredients.
