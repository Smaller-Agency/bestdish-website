"I built this around the cheeses I'd serve a guest at home. The crust is the moment." — Afrim Pristine, Cheese Boutique

Heating instructions

Oven

Preheat to 375°F. Remove lid and cover with foil. Heat for 25–30 minutes. Remove foil and heat an additional 5 minutes for a crisp top.

Microwave

Remove lid. Heat on high for 4–5 minutes. Stir halfway.

Keep frozen until use. Do not refreeze after thawing.

Ingredients

Macaroni pasta, Aged cheddar, Mozzarella, Grana Padano, Parmesan, Whole milk, Cream, Butter, Flour, Onion, Garlic, Mustard powder, Nutmeg, Salt, Black pepper.

Contains: Wheat, Milk.

May contain: Egg, Mustard.

Made in Canada with domestic and imported ingredients.
