The Queen & Beaver

Cheese Burger Pot Pie

This dish takes the core elements of a cheeseburger and rebuilds them inside a pastry shell, creating a structured, balanced version of a familiar comfort food.

Chef

Duncan Simpson

Serving

1 serving (300 g)

Calories

900

Category

Savoury

The chef

"Comfort food works best when it's built properly. This is about balance, not excess."— Duncan Simpson, The Queen & Beaver

Heating instructions

Oven

Preheat to 375°F. Remove lid. Bake 25–30 minutes. Let rest 2 minutes before serving.

Microwave

Not recommended.

Keep frozen until use. Do not refreeze after thawing.

Ingredients

Flour, Ground beef, Cheddar cheese, Beef fat, Shortening, Pickles, Onion, Beef stock, Water, Worcestershire sauce (vinegar, water, molasses, sugar, salt, anchovies, tamarind extract, onions, garlic, spice, natural and artificial flavours), Mustard, White wine vinegar, Egg yolk, Tomato paste, Salt, Garlic, Sesame seeds, Black pepper.

Contains: Wheat, Milk, Fish, Mustard, Egg, Sesame, Sulphites.

May contain: Soybean, Hazelnut and Pecan.

Made in Canada with domestic and imported ingredients.
